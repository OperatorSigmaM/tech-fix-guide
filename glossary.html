Tech Glossary

Don't know what a term means? Find plain English definitions here.

CPU

Central Processing Unit. The "brain" of your computer that handles instructions.

RAM

Random Access Memory. Short-term memory for active tasks.

GPU

Graphics Processing Unit. Handles rendering images and video games.

SSD

Solid State Drive. Fast storage for your files, unlike old HDDs.

VPN

Virtual Private Network. Encrypts your internet connection for privacy.

IP Address

Internet Protocol. A unique address that identifies a device on the internet.
